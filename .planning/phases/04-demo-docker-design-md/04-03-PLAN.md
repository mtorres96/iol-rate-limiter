Source: 02-CONTEXT.

Delta-seconds reset-header convention — all reset/retry headers in delta-seconds (NOT epoch): Retry-After = ceil(retryAfterMs/1000), RateLimit reset = ceil(resetMs/1000), X-RateLimit-Reset also delta-seconds; Retry-After clamped ≥1 on a 429; both IETF and legacy families emitted on allowed AND rejected.
